Common questions

How do I import a recipe?

Paste a web link, choose a photo, or share a video to Foodie, and the app will turn it into a structured recipe.

How do I manage my subscription?

Foodie Pro is billed through your Apple ID. To view, change, or cancel it, open the Settings app on your device, tap your name, then tap Subscriptions. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore a purchase?

In Foodie, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases."
